My three favourite movies

A brief list of my favourite movies

Independence Day

I'm always wondering why I love this movie so much, probably because of my childhood i guess, but it's some sort of comfort movie

I, Robot

I love this movie for its atmosphere, the philosophy behind the Three Laws of Robotics and the evolution of human/robot relationships

The Great Gatsby

That's the story that always breaks my heart, seeing how Gatsby's life was controlled by his unrequited love for Daisy is always heartbreaking.
